Frequently Asked Questions

What happens during the free consultation?

We'll have a brief 15-minute phone conversation where you can share what brings you to therapy, I'll explain my approach, and we can determine if we're a good fit to work together.

Do you accept insurance?

I'm currently out-of-network with insurance, but I can provide superbills for potential reimbursement from your insurance company. I recommend contacting your provider about out-of-network benefits.

What are your session fees?

50-minute sessions are $150. I offer a sliding scale for those who need financial assistance - please don't hesitate to discuss this during our consultation

Where are you located?

I provide services throughout the Denver Metro area. I offer both in-person sessions at my office and secure telehealth sessions via a HIPAA-compliant platform.
